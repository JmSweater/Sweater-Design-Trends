## 🛠 Trend Deep Dive: The Core Anchors (1-3)

### 01. Quarter-Zip & Polo Knits
**Market Status:** #1 Trending Silhouette
- **Technical Specs:** 
  - Placket: 1/4 Zip (YKK/High-polish hardware) or 3-button Polo neck.
  - Gauge: Fine to medium (12GG - 14GG).
  - Material: Merino wool / Cashmere blends.
- **Implementation Advice:**
  - Focus on **Cerulean Blue** for social media engagement.
  - Standardize fit to "Relaxed-Straight" to capture the "unisex" buyer segment.

### 02. Minimalist Rollneck & Turtleneck
**Market Status:** High-Conversion Layering Piece
- **Technical Specs:**
  - Weight: 200–250 gsm (Spring-friendly).
  - Collar: 2-inch minimum height (floppy or folded).
- **SEO/Product Page Focus:**
  - Keywords: `Anti-pilling`, `Machine Washable`, `Breathable`.
- **Strategy:** Position as a "second-skin" base layer for blazer pairings.

<div align="center"><img src="https://jmsweater.com/uploads/202658/20260508152845654565.webp" style="width:400px" alt="Minimalist Rollneck & Turtleneck"></div>

### 03. Textured & Cable-Knit Popovers
**Market Status:** High-Margin Premium Item
- **Technical Specs:**
  - Front-panel cable or basket-stitch texture.
  - Ribbed cuffs/hem with high elasticity recovery.
- **Profitability Profile:** 
  - Perceived value allows for a **30%–50% price markup** over flat-knit basics.
  - High ROI on visual assets (3D textures perform 40% better in TikTok/Reels ads).
