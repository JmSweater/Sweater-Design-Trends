## 🛠 Trend Deep Dive: The Traffic Drivers (4-6)

### 04. Dopamine Brights (Primary & Bold Pastel)
**Market Status:** The "Scroll-Stopper" for Social Commerce.
- **Technical Specs:**
  - High-saturation dyeing: Cobalt Blue, Lemon Yellow, Bright Red.
  - Yarn: Lightweight cotton-acrylic blends or viscose for vibrant color retention.
- **Implementation Advice:**
  - Use these SKUs as **Hero Images** in top-of-funnel (TOFU) ads.
  - Even if stock is limited, these colors reduce Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) by driving organic clicks.

### 05. Knit-on-Knit Pre-layered Designs
**Market Status:** High-Value "Instant Style" Solution.
- **Technical Specs:**
  - Integrated "fake" collars and hems.
  - Mixed stitch patterns (e.g., cable body with rib-knit mock layers).
- **Execution Strategy:**
  - Ideal for **Holiday Gifting** and "Effortless Fashion" collections.
  - **QA Warning:** Pay critical attention to the tension at joining seams to prevent tearing during wear.

### 06. Touch-Me Textures & Fancy Stitches
**Market Status:** Premium Perception through Visual Depth.
- **Technical Specs:**
  - Openwork, lace stitches, pointelle, and 3D "bubble" knits.
  - Mixed yarn thickness to create topographical depth.
- **Conversion Strategy:**
  - Use high-resolution macro photography to communicate "touchability" digitally.
  - These styles justify a **premium price point** due to higher machine-hour requirements.
